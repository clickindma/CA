Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and processes.

What is GST and who needs to register for it?

GST (Goods and Services Tax) is an indirect tax levied on the supply of goods and services. Businesses with an annual turnover exceeding ₹20 lakhs (₹10 lakhs for special category states) must register for GST. Additionally, businesses involved in interstate supply, e-commerce operations, or those required to pay tax under reverse charge must register regardless of turnover.

How long does it take to incorporate a company in India?

The typical timeframe for company incorporation in India is 7-15 working days, depending on the type of entity and complexity. This includes obtaining Director Identification Number (DIN), Digital Signature Certificates (DSC), name approval, and certificate of incorporation. With our expert assistance, we ensure the process is completed efficiently with minimal delays.

What are the benefits of UDYAM/MSME registration?

UDYAM/MSME registration provides numerous benefits including priority sector lending, lower interest rates, subsidies on patent registration, protection against delayed payments, exemption from direct tax laws for startups, concession in electricity bills, and eligibility for various government schemes and incentives designed to promote small businesses in India.

What is the due date for filing Income Tax Returns?

For individuals and entities not requiring audit, the due date is typically July 31 following the end of the financial year. For businesses requiring audit, the due date is October 31. For companies with international transactions, it's November 30. However, these dates may be extended by the Income Tax Department. We recommend filing early to avoid last-minute issues.

How often do I need to file GST returns?

Regular GST taxpayers must file monthly or quarterly returns depending on their turnover and scheme opted for. GSTR-1 (sales details) and GSTR-3B (summary return) are the primary returns. Composition scheme taxpayers file quarterly returns. Annual returns (GSTR-9) must be filed by all GST-registered entities. We provide comprehensive GST filing services to ensure compliance with all deadlines.

What services do you provide for ROC compliance?

Our ROC compliance services include annual filings (AOC-4, MGT-7), managing changes in company details (directors, registered office, share capital), maintaining statutory registers, conducting board/general meetings, and preparing minutes. We ensure timely compliance with all Registrar of Companies requirements to avoid penalties and maintain your company's good standing.
